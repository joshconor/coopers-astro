PHOTO INBOX
===========

Drop any photos here from Finder. Don't worry about naming — Claude will rename and move them when you describe them in chat.

To use a photo on the site, after dropping it here, tell Claude in chat:
  - the filename
  - where it goes (hero / product / story / gallery / about)
  - a short name
  - alt text (one sentence describing what's in the image)

That's it. Claude takes care of the rest.

Folder map (after Claude processes):
  hero/      → big hero section photos
  products/  → photos that go on product cards (e.g., the missing pig photos)
  farm/      → lifestyle / story / about / gallery photos
