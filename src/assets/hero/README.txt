HERO PHOTOS GO HERE
===================

Drop hero images in this folder. Recommended:
- hero-main.jpg     — primary photo shown on the right side of the hero
- hero-bg.jpg       — optional background photo (full-bleed behind the headline)

Sizes:
- hero-main: at least 1200×1200 (square works best)
- hero-bg:   at least 2400×1400

Formats: .jpg, .png, or .webp

After dropping a file, tell Claude its filename and I'll wire it in.
